Why Ukraine is justified in its stance

Ukraine's stance is justified for several reasons:

  • 1. Territorial integrity: Ukraine has the right to protect its territorial integrity and sovereignty.
  • 2. International law: Russia's actions in annexing Crimea and supporting separatist movements in eastern Ukraine violate international law.
  • 3. Self-determination: The people of Ukraine have the right to determine their own political future without external interference.
  • 4. Support from the international community: Many countries have expressed support for Ukraine and condemned Russia's actions.
